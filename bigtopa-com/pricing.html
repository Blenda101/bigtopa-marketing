Under the BigTopa All you need to
Go Virtual

Spaces

Create dynamic virtual environments with BigTopa Spaces. Tailor them for various interactions, from focused work sessions to large community gatherings, all within a unified platform.

Stream

Facilitate ongoing dialogue and collaboration in real-time. BigTopa Streams are the lifelines of your Spaces, keeping conversations active and accessible.

Room

BigTopa Rooms are sub-environments within your Spaces, allowing for targeted discussions and niche meetups, complete with bespoke privacy settings.

Provide Spaces

Customized spaces for professionals to conduct one-on-one consultations and services, featuring tools for scheduling, payments, and personalized client management.

Work Spaces

Collaborative areas designed for teams to converge, create, and share. Work Spaces are equipped with productivity and project management tools to foster efficiency and innovation.

Community Spaces

Open and inclusive spaces for communities to thrive. Host forums, share updates, and engage members in a space that's built for connectivity and growth.

Event Spaces

Versatile spaces for hosting virtual events. From planning to execution, Event Spaces provide the functionalities you need for a successful gathering, complete with registration and attendee interaction tools.

Meetups

Transform your virtual gatherings with BigTopa Meetups, offering flexible formats for any type of engagement, whether it's a small workshop or a large conference.

Meets

Conduct intimate, one-on-one meetings in a private and secure setting. Meets are ideal for deep, personal connections and focused discussions.

Meetings

BigTopa Meetings bring your team or clients together in a collaborative space, perfect for brainstorming, presenting, and making collective decisions.

Webinars

Educate and engage with BigTopa Webinars. Share your knowledge, interact with a large audience, and provide valuable content in an immersive learning environment.

Brodcast

Reach out to a wider audience with BigTopa Broadcasts. Stream your events live, engage with viewers, and deliver high-quality content on a scalable platform.
