Space components

Every space is built
from the same four components.

Not every space activates every component. The platform composes what each space type needs — nothing more, nothing less.

Streams

The messaging layer. Always on in every space — but behavior adapts. Work spaces sort by recency and task relevance. Community spaces surface trending content. Event spaces follow a timeline.

All space types · Always on
Meetups

Five meeting types, each composable per plan: Meet Now (instant), Meet (scheduled 1-on-1), Meetings (multi-participant), Webinars (host-to-audience), Broadcast (studio streaming). No space needs all five.

Optional · Composable per plan
Library

The knowledge layer. Notes, tasks, posts, files, and canvas — all tied to the context where they were created. A decision made in a meeting stays connected to the meeting stream that generated it.

Work & Community spaces
Rooms

Focused areas within a space. A project room within a work space. A committee room within a community space. Rooms maintain their own streams, meetups, and library with separate access controls.

Configurable per space type
