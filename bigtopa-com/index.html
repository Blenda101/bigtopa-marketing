Fit

The platform adapts
to your organization.
Not the other way around.

Most platforms make you adapt to them. BigTopa's entitlement system composes a different platform for every organization type.

Composable entitlements

Features are assembled per organization, not bundled into rigid tiers. A Greek chapter gets different capabilities than a reunion organizer — even on the same plan level.

Zero code changes per market

Adding a new market vertical doesn't require a new codebase. The entitlement registry changes. The application code is identical across every market.

Hundreds of compositions possible

From a simple chat-only family hub to a full creator platform with monetization and broadcasting — even nomenclature-only changes create distinct products.

Context doesn't get lost when
people move.

In most platforms, when you transition from one tool to another, you start over. BigTopa preserves context as activity moves through the platform.

01
Information Flow

Conversations that start in a project room surface important updates to the space hub — without broadcasting noise to everyone.

02
Activity Flow

Moving from async discussion to a live meeting happens within the same continuous platform — not by switching tools and losing thread.

03
Context Flow

A meeting that started from a discussion stays connected to it. Decisions remain tied to their originating context. Revenue flows room-up to the hub.
