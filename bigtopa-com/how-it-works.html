The movement principle

Flow — content and revenue
move where they should.

BigTopa has built-in flow between Rooms, Spaces, and the Hub. Some things flow up naturally. Some flow down by design. Participants move both ways. You control the direction.

Origin
Room
Where activity begins. Focused, contextual, intimate. Every ticket sold, every conversation started — it begins here.
