Space

Tailor Your Universe with BigTopa Spaces

BigTopa Spaces are designed to cater to every facet of your virtual engagement needs. Each Space type combines streams, meetups and library resources to serve a unique purpose.

01.

Provider Spaces

Provider Spaces are customized for one-on-one interactions, with private rooms tailored for each client. Enabling providers to facilitate consulta-tions, coaching, and personalized services in a confidential setting.

02.

Work Spaces

Ideal for internal team collaborations and project work. Work Spaces are provisioned to be your go-to for sustainable productivity and organizing engagement to work done.

03.

Community Spaces

Build and nurture your community. These spaces are equipped for engaging your audience over shared interests that generate mutual value and community growth.

04.

Event Spaces

Host your events with flair. From webinars to conferences, Event Spaces provide all the tools you need to create memorable experiences including marketing and monitization.

Feature Highlights

Customizable

Settings adapt each Space to fit your needs, whether it’s branding, features, or functionality.

Rooms

Seamlessly connected sub-spaces tailored for focused engagements, equip with same features as the main Space.

Integration

Seamlessly incorporate external tools and services through the apps feature, enhancing your Space without the clutter.

Each Space in BigTopa is a world of possibilities, waiting for you to make it your own. Ready to create, engage, and inspire? Start shaping your Space today.

Meetups

Connect and Engage with BigTopa Meetups

BigTopa Meetups bring your virtual connections to life, offering a range of formats for audio video communication to suit every type of gathering:

01.

Meet

Optimized for intimate one-on-one sessions, allowing for deep, personal connections. Utilized for Direction Messages (DM) and appointment booking capable Provider Spaces.

02.

Meetings

Ideal for collaborative team efforts, providing a space for ideas to flourish. Facilitate group meetings with multiple participants, each capable of presenting and interacting.

03.

Webinars

Intended for situations where a small group host engage a larger audience with presentations, ensuring everyone gets a front-row experience.

04.

Broadcasts

Expand your reach with high-quality, large-scale streaming to participants far and wide. Broadcast production studio tools enable both recorded and live streaming to Facebook, YouTube, and other platforms.

Feature Highlights

Versatility

Choose the format that fits your engagement needs, from one-on-one meetings to massive broadcasts.

Interactive Tools

Polls, Q&A, and screen layout options that enrich participant engagement.

Seamless Integration

Seamlessly incorporate Meetups into your Spaces, creating a cohesive experience.

BigTopa Meetups are designed to make virtual gatherings engaging, manageable, and memorable, ensuring that value is optimized for every participant.

Library

Organize and Access with BigTopa Library

BigTopa Library is your ultimate resource within Spaces, streamlining the organization and access to conversation, people, tools, and valued content.

01.

Collections

Curate and manage content access with ease, creating collections that house stream accessible content, private settings require granting access.

02.

Blogs

Effortlessly create, share, and engage with blog posts directly within the stream, enhancing the richness and clarity of content in discussions.

03.

Files

Easily add, access and preview documents and media, enriching conversations with relevant content, all organized within your library collection for quick retrieval.

04.

DM (Direct Messaging)

Seamlessly message any participant within your space, fostering private, impactful conversations that deepen connections. Isolate and access any content shared with an individual Space participant.

05.

Search and Filter

Find exactly what you need with advanced search and filtering options, making it effortless to navigate through vast amounts of content.

06.

Saved Item

Pin stream conversations and content to this special collection for easy recall and retrieval.

07.

Rooms

Setup each Space with room to break out activities for more focused engagement. Rooms are provisioned with library and meetup features found in main Spaces.

08.

Apps and Integration

Seamlessly integrate BigTopa modules and third party apps into Space Streams and Meetups, ensuring relevant resources are always at hand for every engagement.

Feature Highlights

Centralized Access

Keep all your space’s content in one organized, easily navigable place.

Dynamic Categorization

Automatically sort content into default library collections based on type or activity.

Paywall Content

Grant access to private collections through payments, event tickets or subscription plans.

The BigTopa Library redefines content management in virtual spaces, making it simple to organize, find, and utilize resources, ensuring your community or team has everything they need right at their fingertips.
