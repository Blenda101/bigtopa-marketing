Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Effective date] · AlumniHub is operated by [Legal entity name] ("AlumniHub," "we," "us").

AlumniHub helps alumni organizations run giving, events, and community. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have — for both the organizations that use AlumniHub and the alumni and donors they serve.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide and information generated as you use the platform:

  • Account & organization data — names, email addresses, roles, and organization details for administrators and volunteers.
  • Member & donor data — contact details, class year, chapter or region, giving history, and event registrations that an organization manages in AlumniHub.
  • Donation & payment data — gift amounts, designations, and receipts. Card and bank details are processed by our payment provider (see below); we do not store full card numbers.
  • Content you create — posts, announcements, messages, photos, and documents within a chapter hub.
  • Usage & device data — log data, device and browser information, and cookies used to operate and improve the service.

How we use information

  • To provide and operate the platform — campaigns, donations, events, communications, and community features.
  • To process donations and send receipts.
  • To enable organizations to communicate with their members and donors (organizations control these communications).
  • To secure the service, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations.
  • To improve features and support.

Donations & payments

Payments are processed by [Stripe] and other selected providers. Their handling of payment information is governed by their own privacy terms. AlumniHub receives transaction metadata (amount, status, designation) needed to record gifts, issue receipts, and report to the organization.

Organizations as data controllers

When an alumni organization uses AlumniHub to manage its members, donors, and campaigns, that organization is the controller of its data and AlumniHub acts as a processor on its behalf. Requests to access, correct, or delete member or donor data should be directed to the relevant organization; we assist organizations in fulfilling those requests.

Chapter Hub Connect & Flow

Chapters are independent. Information moves between connected chapters only through Chapter Hub Connect, which is opt-in and consented on both sides, and only for the domains (Financial, Content, Events, People) each chapter has enabled. A chapter can review or end a connection at any time. We do not share data between unconnected organizations.

Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We share it only with: service providers who help us operate the platform (under contract); the organization whose hub the data belongs to; and authorities where required by law. AlumniHub does not display third-party advertising.

Cookies

We use necessary cookies to run the service and, where permitted, analytics cookies to understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as an organization's account is active and as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Organizations can request export or deletion of their data subject to legal record-keeping requirements (for example, donation records).

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact the organization that holds your data, or reach us at [privacy@alumnihub.co] and we will route your request appropriately.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — including encryption in transit and at rest and access controls — to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and to notify affected parties of incidents as required by law.

Children

AlumniHub is intended for organizations and adults. It is not directed to children under [13/16], and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the "last updated" date; material changes will be communicated as required.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at [privacy@alumnihub.co] or [mailing address].
