Financial transparency

Trust is the
real endowment.

Most associations are independent 501(c)(3)s raising for the institution. AlumniHub makes every gift visible and every transfer accountable — so the conversation stays about impact, not bookkeeping.

✓
Donor-facing impact. Every donor sees the goal, the total raised, and exactly what their gift supports.
✓
Organizer accountability. A full ledger of gifts in and funds disbursed — auditable at any time.
✓
Clean handoff to the institution. One report shows exactly what to transfer, when, and on whose behalf.
