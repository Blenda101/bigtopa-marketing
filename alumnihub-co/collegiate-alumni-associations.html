What associations run

More than a fund.
A year-round organization.

Associations don't just collect gifts — they convene alumni. AlumniHub carries the whole calendar, with giving woven through all of it.

💛
The annual fund & campaigns
Run the annual fund across every chapter and class year, with matching gifts, challenge grants, and live participation tracking that drives friendly competition between chapters.
🎤
Conferences & signature events
Host the national conference, regional galas, and homecoming — ticketing, sponsorships, and registration built in, every dollar tied back to the cause.
📍
Chapter & regional events
Give each city chapter the tools to run its own mixers, game-watch parties, and service days — independently, under one association brand.
🗂️
Members across decades
A living directory spanning every graduating class and every region — so outreach reaches the right alumni, wherever they've moved.
Chapter Hub Flow

National, regional, local —
connected on your terms.

An association is a family of independent chapters. With Chapter Hub Connect, they link into a structure up to three levels deep — funds and totals roll up to the national fund, brand and campaigns roll down, and members move between cities when they relocate. Every connection is opt-in and consented.
