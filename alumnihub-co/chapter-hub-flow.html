Independent chapters.
Connected when you choose.

Every chapter is its own hub, with its own members, money, and events. Chapter Hub Connect links chapters into a family. Chapter Hub Flow moves what you allow between them — opt-in by domain, consented on both sides, never automatic.

What it is

Flow happens within —
and, when you connect, between.

Chapter Hub Flow is the movement of value through your organization. Inside a single chapter it just works. Across chapters, it travels only over a connection you've opted into.

Within a chapter
Flow inside your hub

Content, gifts, events, and people move naturally between your chapter's rooms and spaces. This is core to every chapter, on every tier — no connection required.

Between chapters
Flow across the family

Once chapters are connected, the same flow can cross between them — funds rolling up to a regional or national hub, brand and campaigns cascading down. Enabled by Chapter Hub Connect.

Two capabilities

Connect is the relationship.
Flow is the movement.

They work together: Connect establishes a known, consented link between two chapters; Flow is what travels across it, domain by domain.

The link
Chapter Hub Connect

The premium feature that links chapters into a family. A child chapter opts in and connects up to a parent; both sides see and agree to the connection. Without Connect, a chapter stands alone.

The movement
Chapter Hub Flow

What moves across a connection — Financial, Content, Events, and People — each turned on independently, with its own settings. Flow within a chapter is always on; flow between chapters rides on Connect.

Privacy & security

Consent first.
Connections are known.

Independent 501(c)(3)s guard their own donor lists. Chapter Hub Flow is built so nothing is shared silently or by default.

🤝
Opt-in, both sides

A connection only exists when both chapters agree to it. Either side can see it, review it, and turn it off.

🔒
Granular control

Each of the four domains is its own switch with its own settings. Share a calendar without ever sharing a donor record.

🛡️
Standards-backed

Encryption in transit and at rest, audit trails on every connection, and privacy controls that meet nonprofit data standards.

The institution

A dotted or solid line
to the university.

Associations relate to their institution in different ways. AlumniHub makes that one edge configurable.

Dotted line
Independent

A separate 501(c)(3) with its own board, raising for the university. Funds transfer as deliberate, recorded disbursements — the institution is a connected party, not a parent.

Solid line
Integrated

Run inside the university's Advancement office. Funds and reporting flow directly to the institution as part of the family.
